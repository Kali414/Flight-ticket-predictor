Predicting Airplane Ticket Prices

The price of airplane tickets depends on various factors such as:

  • Airline
  • Date of Journey
  • Source
  • Destination
  • Departure Time
  • Arrival Time
  • Duration
  • Total Stops
  • Additional Information

By analyzing these factors, you can predict the ticket prices more accurately.
